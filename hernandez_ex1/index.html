History

Chess is a game that has been around for a very long time. It is believed that the game was invented in India around 600 AD. The game was then brought to Persia and then to Europe. The game was played on a board that was made of ivory. Its pieces were made of ivory and gold. Chess was very popular in Europe. It was played in the courts of royalties. They would play the game for hours. The game was and is still very popular in Europe. It is also very popular in the United States. The game is played by people of all ages. It is a game that is played by many people all over the world.

The Battleground

The battleground for a jalous fit for two kings is the chessboard. The two sides are on the opposite of each other with the goal of overtaking the king of the opposite party. Clasically and for most of the time, there are two sides: whites, and blacks. The whites are located on rows 1-2 on the board. While the blacks are located on rows 7-8 on the board. The board uses numbers and letters to keep track of the game using chess notation.
